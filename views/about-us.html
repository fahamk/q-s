About Us

VISION

Work of God enhance the understanding of the word of God. To explore signs لَآيَاتٍ in the working of the universe

MISSION

  1. Explore how working of the universe confirm the truth given in the Quran
    سَنُرِيهِمْ آيَاتِنَا فِي الْآفَاقِ وَفِي أَنفُسِهِمْ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَهُمْ أَنَّهُ الْحَقّ41/53: We shall show them Our signs in the horizons and within themselves, until, it will become clear to them that whatever the Quran had told them was based on truth.
  2. To advance exploitation free way of living, based on the role model of The Prophet PBUH as documented in Quran
  3. Create awareness that, Same as the Laws of nature control the universe, the Laws of deeds (given in the Quran) control the results to human deeds

About Us

The Quran with Science provides a common platform to explore the signs of Allah in the design of the Universe. Quran & Science is to empower Muslims with the knowledge of The Quran,
The is a setup of educated rational minded muslims with no affiliation to any sect and with no economic motive. Every one with us is a volunteer.

Quran with Science is an academic forum for the understanding of the Quran from the rational prospective, free from pre-conceived ideas. It is open to all muslims of the world to empower themselves with the knowledge of Quran, to create awareness for 100% authenticity in their knowledge, to encourage rational and lateral thinking and to discover how the work of Allah enhance the understanding of the Word of Allah.

INSPIRATION

We are inspired by the Quran Sura 3 Ayah 189-190-191
وَلِلَّـهِ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۗ وَاللَّـهُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ Allah Who is supreme over all universe and has devised measures for all things.
إِنَّ فِي خَلْقِ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَاخْتِلَافِ اللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ لَآيَاتٍ لِّأُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ In the creation of the earth and the heavens and the alternation of night and day, there are signs for those gifted with understanding
الَّذِينَ يَذْكُرُونَ اللَّـهَ قِيَامًا وَقُعُودًا وَعَلَىٰ جُنُوبِهِمْ وَيَتَفَكَّرُونَ فِي خَلْقِ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ These people keep Allah in mind whilst standing, sitting or reclining. After reflecting upon the creation of the heavens and the earth رَبَّنَا مَا خَلَقْتَ هَـٰذَا بَاطِلًا سُبْحَانَكَ فَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ Our Rabb, You have not created this Universe in vain. Glory to you. Save us from punishment of fire

RATIONAL APPROACH

We start with the in-depth rational understanding the The Quran, integrate the Quran with the world knowledge, respect to all in a sect free environment, and create awareness among muslims to achieve the status of ADAM and status of Momin by subduing forces of nature, hence to learn natural sciences are integral part of Islam:
وَسَخَّرَ لَكُم مَّا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ جَمِيعًا مِّنْهُ ۚ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يَتَفَكَّرُونَ(45/13: He has subdued everything in the heavens and on earth for you, within the bounds of His Law. And herein are signs for those who think and reflect)
