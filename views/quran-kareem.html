Quran Translations

Word of God is in original Arabic. Translations of Quran are not called Quran but just the translations of Quran by a named Translator.

Translations are dependent upon:

  • Arabic language Knowledge of the translator
  • Contemporary knowledge of the translator
  • Pre-occupation or religion of the translator

A Good translation is:

  • Consistent - free from contradictions
  • Free from ambiguities
  • Unbiased un-preconceived mind set - no undue links
  • Without bracket additions, original word in mind
  • Minimum outside Quran explanations
