DEEN

82/17 Who can tell you how the Day of Deen will be?

82/18.   Of course who else but Allah the Almighty, can tell you how that period would be?

82/19.   That will be the Day when everyone will see his deeds in front of him. Neither will anyone be able to do anything for the other; nor would one have any authority over the other. All power and authority will rest with the Divine Laws; and only these laws will prevail. (In other words, man will neither be subservient to any other, nor will he be needy. Definition of Muslim as per Quran Sura 2/177

لَّيْسَ الْبِرَّ أَن تُوَلُّوا وُجُوهَكُمْ قِبَلَ الْمَشْرِقِ وَالْمَغْرِبِ وَلَـٰكِنَّ الْبِرَّ مَنْ آمَنَ بِاللَّـهِ وَالْيَوْمِ الْآخِرِ وَالْمَلَائِكَةِ وَالْكِتَابِ وَالنَّبِيِّينَ وَآتَى الْمَالَ عَلَىٰ
True piety does not consist in turning your faces towards the east or the west - but truly pious is he who believes in God, and the Last Universe; and the malaika, and revelation, and the prophets; and spends his substance …..

Five Point Believe to become a Muslim

  1. Believe in God
  2. Believe in the life in the next world
  3. Believe in the Forces of nature (Malaika)
  4. Believe in the revelation on each prophet (Protected & complete Quran supercedes all earlier revelations )
  5. Believe in all Prophets

Note: Some time 'predetermination of destiny' is added as sixth point. It is not mentioned in Quran. Any addition from sources outside Quran is in clash with Quranic spirit and is example of lack of research in the religion.

Believe in God Includes that:

God is watching human mind and deeds. It is a believe in central law in Quran as law of cause and effect of human deeds and choices. The law of cause and effect is coupled with law of respite. It mean the results generated by the law of cause and effect are delayed for a specific period. As soon as the period ends, the results catch the person. The people with heavy side of Good deeds on scale, will create jennet society for living in this world and will get great success in life in the next world after physical body death. The next world (Parallel World with different laws of nature) will be so different that our minds in this world can not grasp its nature.

NO FALSE ACCUSE

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَرْمُونَ الْمُحْصَنَاتِ الْغَافِلَاتِ الْمُؤْمِنَاتِ لُعِنُوا فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ عَظِيمٌ

Ascertain before accepting any slanderous information
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِن جَاءَكُمْ فَاسِقٌ بِنَبَإٍ فَتَبَيَّنُوا أَن تُصِيبُوا قَوْمًا بِجَهَالَةٍ فَتُصْبِحُوا عَلَىٰ مَا فَعَلْتُمْ نَادِمِينَ
49/6 O Rasool! Tell your companions, "If any deviant person comes to you with a (slanderous) tale, do not immediately accept it. First ascertain it, lest you unwittingly take a step which brings harm to someone; and then regret what you have done

[17:36] And do not pursue that of which you have no knowledge. Indeed, the hearing, the sight and the heart - about all those [one] will be questioned.

Show a positive attitude about your own people and should have responded: This appears to be an accusation.

[24:12] "When you heard this rumour, why was your first reaction not like that of righteous men and women? You should have shown a positive attitude about your own people and should have responded: This appears to be an accusation." ?

Do not backbite against others
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اجْتَنِبُوا كَثِيرًا مِّنَ الظَّنِّ إِنَّ بَعْضَ الظَّنِّ إِثْمٌ ۖ وَلَا تَجَسَّسُوا وَلَا يَغْتَب بَّعْضُكُم بَعْضًا ۚ أَيُحِبُّ أَحَدُكُمْ أَن يَأْكُلَ لَحْمَ أَخِيهِ مَيْتًا فَكَرِهْتُمُوهُ ۚ وَاتَّقُوا اللَّـهَ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّـهَ تَوَّابٌ رَّحِيمٌ
49/12. (When mutual differences arise, mischievous people take advantage and tell tales to both parties. In this regard you should be very careful. Have positive feelings and) Avoid ill feelings against others. Furthermore some ill feelings slacken or dull goodwill. And do not eavesdrop or backbite against one another. Would any one of you like to eat the flesh of a dead brother? You would abhor it!

Never Ridicule; Never Scandalize, Never be Sarcastic and call offensive names
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا يَسْخَرْ قَوْمٌ مِّن قَوْمٍ عَسَىٰ أَن يَكُونُوا خَيْرًا مِّنْهُمْ وَلَا نِسَاءٌ مِّن نِّسَاءٍ عَسَىٰ أَن يَكُنَّ خَيْرًا مِّنْهُنَّ ۖ وَلَا تَلْمِزُوا أَنفُسَكُمْ وَلَا تَنَابَزُوا بِالْأَلْقَابِ ۖ بِئْسَ الِاسْمُ الْفُسُوقُ بَعْدَ الْإِيمَانِ ۚ وَمَن لَّمْ يَتُبْ فَأُولَـٰئِكَ هُمُ الظَّالِمُونَ
49/11 One faction starts ridiculing the other and tries belittling them. It is quite possible that those being ridiculed might be better than the members of your own party. Neither your men nor women should show such behaviour; nor should you scandalize one another; nor be sarcastic and call offensive names. After professing Eiman you are expected to exhibit exemplary behaviour. You should not address one another illmanneredly. This is very offensive. If someone has acted like that he should immediately regret it and stop this attitude. If he does not do so, according to the Divine Law he would be considered guilty.
